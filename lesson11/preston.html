Preston Area Farmer's Market

Springtime in Preston signals a return of many things, including the return of the Preston Area Farmer's Market. Since 1962, the public has been able to come together every Saturday morning and enjoy the best of what Preston and its surrounding areas have to offer.

Local sellers bring goods that delight and entice, and everything one can think of from fruits and vegetables to home-baked goods and arts and crafts can be bought. In addition, the livestock section of the Market allows buyers and sellers to negotiate and trade in a common location.

Whatever your passion is, it can be found at the Preston Area Farmer's Market. Come check it out, you're guaranteed to find something that you love.

The Preston Area Farmer's Market is every Saturday April to October on 1st Ave. and Jones Street in Preston.
