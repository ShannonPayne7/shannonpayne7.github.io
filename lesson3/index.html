Site Name

Weather Watch Idaho

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share information for current weather conditions in southeastern Idaho, specifically in the towns of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. Current weather and future weather forecasts will be provided, as well as storm alerts in a Storm Center, to give residents and visitors weather information to help them plan for their travel and events. A gallery of images will provide opportunities to share the beauty of the area, and the variety of weather conditions that can be expected. Each town will be able to share what makes them special and unique through spotlighting community information on individual town pages. Access to this weather information and to the town pages will help unite communities and keep them safe, and will be inviting to tourists looking for a new and adventurous getaway.
