Target Audience

Description

The target audience of this site is adults who rely on weather information in order to plan daily activities for themselves and their families. Additionally, the site will target tourists and visitors who may want to travel to the area. The expectations of the site for the audience will are to be able to locate current and accurate weather forecasts in a setting that is easy to navigate and understand.

Personas

Persona 1

Female, age 35 years old, married, works part-time with some college education, has children, average income $65,000 per year. She schedules activities for her family and relies on weather information to plan (how to dress children for school, how long to plan for travel time, what are the roads like, is rain or snow gear needed for sports activities). She uses her phone to access websites, and looks to local news sites and Facebook for information. Her goals are to have her family prepared for their school and sports activities, to be on time to their activities, to travel safely and to know about community family events.

Persona 2

Retired male age 67 with a college degree, average income $50,000 per year. He spends summers in the area with his wife, activities include fishing, boating and hiking. He uses his laptop computer to access websites, and obtains information from local news sites and outdoor activities websites. His goals are to have access to accurate weather information, both current and future forecasts to plan outdoor activities, and to get local town information for upcoming events and activities.

Scenarios

  • Will there be rain during soccer practice?
  • How much snow will fall during the night?
  • Will the water on the lake be calm enough for fishing?
  • Will the weather update be current in my area?
  • What is the average daily high temperature in July?
